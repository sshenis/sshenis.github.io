Building Your Cookie

Cooki is an interactive Desktop/Phone Web Application created by D3 students: Shenis Macabenta, Irvin Alcira, and Naomi Okada from British Columbia institute of Technology.

Cooki’s main purpose is to help users create their “perfect” cookie in a convenient and fun way.

Users also have Cooki’s wide variety of recipies to look at at their disposal.

Building Your Cookie

Access our Build your own cookie feature through the Home page. Then, choose your desired flavour and press Next.

Building Your Cookie
